    ABOUT US

    PACIFIC PAINTING

    OUR VISION

    Our mission is to teach people and show them the easiest and most efficient way to paint miniatures. We aim to give you simple step by step methods that you can use to paint like the pros in no time. If you ever wondered how others were able to make their miniatures look so amazing but couldnt quite figure it out, then look no further, we got you covered.

    THE HOW

    We will show you the most effective ways to paint through pictures and quick, clear, and consise text to follow along. We will show you every step of the way including steps that alot of people dont know or miss.

    THE WHY

    When we first got into collecting and painting miniatures it was a nightmare, no matter what we tried or did, it never seemed to turn out the way we wanted it to. We remember the struggle, and we want to show you what we have learned over the years so you dont have to waste any time making mistakes and be able to learn everything that goes into painting.

    ALL YOU NEED

    We will give you tips and tricks to master painting. We will discuss what you will need in each section to acheive what you are trying to accomplish so youre not left thinking "what are they using?". We will make sure that you will know everything so you are not left with questions and confusion



    DONT BE GRAY START PAINTING TODAY

    This course is desinged to take you from wondering how the pros are doing it, to being a pro yourself. By the time you finish this tutorial you will be eager to get to the battlefield just so you can show off your army. I guarentee everyone will be asking you how you did it. We tried to make this as simple and as quick as possible with very easy steps to follow giving you a smooth pace without any questions or confusion. We leave nothing out, and look forward to sharing everything we know with you.
